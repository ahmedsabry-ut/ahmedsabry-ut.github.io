After a hectic Spring 2024 semester from Dubai, I decided to take a break from taking classes for the next semester. I thought it was worth mentioning my breaks too in these short blog posts just in case I gave off an impression that I was constantly working, studying, and doing life at the same time for 3 straight years, which wasn't the case.

![Break meme](/assets/images/memes/break.jpeg)

I actually had a few good reasons to take a break too, namely I was looking to move to Toronto from Vancouver and that my best friend's wedding was in July in Jordan. Just the thought of having to juggle studying classes with packing/moving/travelling was ... ugh.

It's always good to have a sort of sense of "can I do this/do I have the time/energy for it/can I manage it with everything I have going". I'm not always the best at gauging how much time and energy I feel like I have, especially when I'm planning for something that's going to take 4 months, but I knew that the extent of my energy wouldn't accommodate for classes, and I'm happy I didn't overextend myself either :)
